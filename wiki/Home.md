## 📖 Glossary

**Key Terms:**

- **Arbitrage** - Profiting from price differences across markets
- **Bid-Ask Spread** - Difference between buying and selling prices
- **Commodity** - Raw material or primary product
- **Derivative** - Financial instrument derived from underlying asset
- **Forward Contract** - Agreement for future delivery at fixed price
- **Futures** - Standardized forward contracts traded on exchanges
- **Hedging** - Risk mitigation through offsetting positions
- **Leverage** - Using borrowed capital to increase position size
- **Margin** - Deposit required to enter a trade
- **Settlement** - Completion of a transaction
- **Spot Price** - Current market price for immediate delivery
- **Volatility** - Measure of price fluctuations
